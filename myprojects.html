PayCalc

PayCalc is a C# application used to calculate net pay on a payslip. After accepting some user inputs such as hourly pay and hours worked per week, PayCalc can calculate the net pay by taking into factors like federal income tax, provincial income tax, CPP, etc. You can see the source code here.

Wind Energy Turbine

For the 2014 UBC Physics Olympics, I was tasked to design a wind turbine that could produce the highest energy output in a wind tunnel with a fixed wind speed. My design came 6th place in British Columbia.

VEX Robotics

As part of the 2013 VEX robotics challenge, I built and programmed a robot that could move sandbags around an arena. Points are awarded based on the number of sandbags a robot could move to a designated area. I came in 12th place in Vancouver, BC.

National Crystal Competition

I participated in the 2014 National Crystal Growing Competition. I created a Aluminum Potassium Sulfate crystal that was judged based on quality, size, and shape. I was awarded second place in the Vancouver region for my crystal.
