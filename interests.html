Model United Nations

Model United Nations is a Canada-wide conference that is held every few months for high school and university students to meet and discuss current world problems. I have been to 5 of these conferences, and through that experience, I learned invaluable teamwork and leadership skills by learning how to compromise with others in international diplomacy.
